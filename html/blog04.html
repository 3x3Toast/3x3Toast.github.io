Vacation experiences

Posted on June 16th, 2023 by Tạ Nhật Minh
Noted that everything here including the pictures are not finalized except for the content of the blog post.

Intro

I’m not fond about going to the beach. The dry sand that sticks to your body the moment you touch it. The clear hot sunny sky gazing down over you. And especially the aftermath, where the sand would just get all over your clothes. And you still have to take a shower after already bathing yourself in water?

As you can see, I’m not the biggest fan when it comes to beach holidays, but I’ll still write a recommendation about beach holidays anyway because to make someone like me to recommend a place I personally dislike, would have to be somewhere really good.

The beach

When talking about places with nice beaches, they’d usually talk about Nha Trang. But on holidays those place attracts too many tourists and make the place too crowded to enjoy properly.

But there’s a place with a nice beach, and not many people go there, and that’s in Vung Tau. Not to criticize, but there isn’t much in Vung Tau to attarct tourists. So there’re only the locals on the beach in the summer. Making it a nice place to socialize and get to know better about the people there while enjoying the scenery.

Going to the beach during the day is fun, but I also recommend going there by night too. It could get dangerous because of the high tides, but the experience you get going to the beach at night is really unique.

It makes you feel a sense of emptiness and a bit of existential crisis when seeing such a vast and mysterious view of the beach.

Cost

Another nice thing about beaches without a tourist hotspot is the cost. Not only the local are usually friendly, the prices there are cheap too since it’s mostly sold for the other locals.

Experience

Even though I dislike playing around the beach, lying on a chair and drinking some good coconut water is enough to keep me there all day.

Overall, going there is really chillm and helps you get out of reality and the burden of life for awhile. As NOT a beach enjoyer, I still recommend the place, 10/10 would go there again.
