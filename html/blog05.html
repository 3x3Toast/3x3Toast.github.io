Clothes in different weather

Posted on June 16th, 2023 by Tạ Nhật Minh
What I would wear in different weathers.

Intro

In Vietnam, the weather doesn’t affect so much that I’d have to change clothings a lot, and that’s the one thing good about it.

Normal days

On normal days where there doesn’t wind, rain or get too sunny, I’d just go with a T-shirt and sweatpants when it’s summer, and change the T-shirt with a hoodie when it’s winter. In spring and autumn, I put a windbreaker on because it’d be a little bit more windy at that time.

Hot weather

I really hate the heat, so unless I’m at school and have to follow the school’s dresscode, I’d just wear shorts and a really thin T-shirt.

Cold weather

On cold weather, I alternate between wearing a hoodie and a shirt with a coat on when going out or to school. As an overly self-conscious person, I prefer wearing a hoodie because it gives a certain coziness that makes you feel safe.

Rainy days

I don’t even attempt to go outside when it’s raining at all, but when I have to, other than a raincoat or and umbrella when I’m walking, I often have a windbreaker on to block the rain drops that my umbrella or raincoat can’t block.
