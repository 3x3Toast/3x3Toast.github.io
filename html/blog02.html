Fitness & diet

Posted on June 16th, 2023 by Tạ Nhật Minh
Noted that everything here including the pictures are not finalized except for the content of the blog post.

Intro

As time goes on, people are getting busier and busier with their lives. And I too, is one of those individuals who’s too busy to care about my health.

Fitness

I don’t exercise much, but because of my limited means of transportation, I still do walk around a lot. Going to a bus stop, buying snacks, going to a café,etc… All of them takes at least 20 minutes to get to because everything’s far from each other in Long Bien.

So even though I don’t exercise much, my many trips of going around in a day is hopefully enough to suffice.

Diet

I have a habit of spending too much on food. A trip to a nearby shopping mall usually costs me upward to 80% of the budget I brought. Ice-cream, pastries, drinks, and lots of snacks. Many of them are be stored in my snacks drawer for late night when I have deadlines to finish.

I don’t have breakfast that often, but when I do, it’d often be banh my from the place near my house. I like bread more than rice so I’d buy pastries and sandwich whenever I got the chance to not eat rice.

For lunch, I almost always have a traditional meal consists of rice, meat and vegetable. It’s often the same for dinner too.

But for dinner, I usually try to make things a little bit fancier. Rather than just boiling pork, I try to fry them, or make some other dishes with beef. So at that time I always eat more than what I have for lunch. But I do try to eat more greens in order to balance out the nutrients.

Cheat days

Of course there're cheat days. Whenever there's a celebration going on, or when I'm not in the mood of something, I'd just stop eating traditional meals and have takeouts.

Fortunately there's a bus near my house that takes me directly to HUST's campus. And there having the best value food is not an exaggeration. Kebab, Takoyaki, coffee, etc... They just have everything there. And I don't care how much calories they have on cheat days as long as they're good. Going there also requires me to walk around a lot too so I don't worry too much about getting overweight.
